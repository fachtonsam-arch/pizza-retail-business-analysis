# Pizza Retail Business Analysis

A full-year (2015) sales and operations analysis for a pizza retail business, built in Power BI. The project covers revenue performance, sales trend patterns, menu rankings, ingredient usage, price–volume correlation, and order behaviour.

## Key Findings

- **Friday** is the highest revenue day of the week; **Thanksgiving weekend (27 Nov)** is the single highest day of the year
- **Classic** pizza leads both revenue and volume across all four categories
- A strong **negative price–volume correlation** (r ≈ −1) confirms price elasticity drives purchasing decisions
- The majority of orders are **multi-item**, supporting bundle upsell strategies
- **Mozzarella and Tomato Sauce** appear in every category — critical supply chain items
